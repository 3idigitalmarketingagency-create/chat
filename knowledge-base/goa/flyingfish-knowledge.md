### Dive Sites near Grande Island
1. Grande Island Reef (5-18m) — All cert levels. Healthy coral gardens, moray eels, barracuda, snappers.
2. Suzy's Wreck (12-18m) — OW+ Certified. British-era vessel at ~40ft, soft corals, groupers, stingrays, moray eels.
3. Sail Rock (14-24m) — AOW Required. Rocky pinnacle with strong currents, eagle rays, drift diving.
4. Shelter Cove (6-14m) — All cert levels. Sheltered bay, macro life, nudibranchs.
5. Bounty Bay — Coral reef and sandy bottom.

Marine life: angelfish, parrotfish, snappers, groupers, eagle rays, blue-spotted stingrays, moray eels, hawksbill and olive ridley sea turtles, nudibranchs, crinoids, pipefish.

Water temp: 27-30°C. Visibility: 3-6 metres (varies by season).

## KEY FAQs

### Swimming & Eligibility
- Swimming required? No for beginners — 1:1 instructor throughout. Yes for certification (200m swim, 10-min float).
- Age limit: Minimum 10 years, parental consent under 18. No upper age limit.
- Weight limit: Some practical limits due to gear sizes — declare weight when booking.
- Glasses: Prescription masks available — mention when booking.
- Contact lenses: Soft contacts fine under mask.

### Safety & Medical
- Scared of water? Normal! Pool training first, instructor beside you, signal to surface anytime.
- Medical form: Required for all. Doctor's clearance if on daily medication.
- Asthma: May be possible with mild, well-controlled asthma + doctor's clearance.
- Pregnant: Cannot dive. Welcome on the boat trip for snorkeling.
- Period: Yes, you can dive. Use tampon or menstrual cup.
- Flying after diving: No flying for 18-24 hours after last dive.
- Alcohol: No alcohol 24 hours before diving.
- Insurance: 10 Lakh rupees coverage per person.

### Booking & Payment
- Booking advance: ₹500 non-refundable per person via UPI/bank transfer. Balance on dive day.
- Payment methods: UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, cash, cards.
- Cancellation: 7+ days = full refund minus ₹500. 3-7 days = 50% refund. Less than 3 days = no refund. Weather cancellation = full refund.
- Group discount: Yes — contact on WhatsApp for custom quote.
- Equipment: All included — ScubaPro BCD, regulator, wetsuit, mask, fins, dive computer.
- Non-diving companions: Can join boat and snorkel. Ask about charges.

### Location & Season
- Best time: October–May. Best visibility: February–March.
- Monsoon closure: June–September.
- Dive location: Grande Island, 30 min boat ride from Candolim.
- Departure: From Novotel Resort & Spa, Candolim.
- Marine life: 100+ species — angelfish, parrotfish, snappers, groupers, eagle rays, stingrays, moray eels, turtles.

### Photos & Videos
- HD photos and videos: FREE in ALL packages.
- Delivery: Shared via download link after the dive.
- Own camera: GoPro/action camera may be allowed — check in advance.

### What to Bring
- Swimwear, towel, change of clothes, sunscreen, sunglasses
- Valid government photo ID (mandatory)
- All diving equipment is provided
- Leave valuables and loose jewellery at hotel

### What to Eat
- Light, easy-to-digest meal 1-2 hours before dive
- Avoid heavy, greasy, spicy food
- Stay hydrated with water, avoid carbonated drinks
- No alcohol night before or day of dive
